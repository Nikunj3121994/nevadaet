Extreme heating events in southern CA that occurred in late September, 2010 setting record high temperatures in LA exceeding 110 degrees F. Santa Ana winds are derived from spring or fall cool surface air temperatures in the high desert areas of the Great Basin and Mojave that heat up during descent from the high desert toward the coast.
