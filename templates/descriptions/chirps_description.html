CHIRPS

  • Description:Climate Hazards Group InfraRed Precipitation with Station data (CHIRPS) is a 30+ year quasi-global rainfall dataset
  • Organization:UCSB/CHG
  • Spatial resolution: 4.8-km grid (1/20 deg)
  • Time Span: 1981-present(Updated every month)
  • Variables:
    • 5-day precipitation
