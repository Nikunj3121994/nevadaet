The Rim Fire is the largest wildfire on record in the Sierra Nevada mountain range. The Rim Fire started on August 17, 2013 in a remote canyon in Stanislaus National Forest in the Sierra Nevada, California. The fire burned 1,041.31 km2 (402.053 sq mi). The Rim Fire was fully contained on October 24, 2013. See the following links to explore atmospheric and land surface conditions before and after the fire.
